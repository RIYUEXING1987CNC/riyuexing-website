Inserts & Thread Parts

Product Overview

Precision threaded inserts and threaded components. As an OEM/ODM precision CNC machining manufacturer, RI YUE XING produces inserts & thread parts strictly to customer drawings and specifications — not as standard catalog products. Every part becomes a critical component inside our customers' finished products.

Features & Customer Benefits

  • Excellent thread quality
  • Strong retention
  • Stable dimensions
  • Long service life

Manufacturing Capabilities

Manufactured using Swiss-type CNC turning, CNC turning, CNC milling and 5-axis machining as required, with precision secondary processing (plating, polishing, deburring). See our full manufacturing capabilities.

Typical Applications

  • Plastic injection products
  • Consumer electronics
  • Medical housings
  • Aluminum structures
  • Industrial equipment

Quality Assurance

Every batch is verified through First Article Inspection (FAI), In-Process Inspection, 100% Final Inspection and CMM measurement against your drawing tolerances before shipment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you manufacture parts from our own drawing?

Yes. RI YUE XING is an OEM/ODM manufacturer — we machine to your drawing and specification rather than selling fixed catalog parts. Send us your drawing (2D/3D) and we'll confirm feasibility, materials and tolerances.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ depends on part complexity, material and tooling requirements. We support both smaller trial orders and stable long-term mass production — contact us with your specification for a tailored quote.

What materials and tolerances can you work with?

We machine stainless steel, aluminum, brass, copper, titanium and other engineering metals, holding tolerances as tight as ±0.01mm depending on part geometry.

How is quality verified before shipment?

Every order goes through First Article Inspection (FAI), In-Process Inspection, 100% Final Inspection and CMM measurement against your drawing tolerances.
