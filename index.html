About Us

A trusted Taiwan CNC manufacturer since 1987

Established in 1987 and located in Kaohsiung, Taiwan, RI YUE XING is a leader in manufacturing CNC lathe components with our own CNC composite processing facility. We operate travelling-spindle CNC lathes, travelling tools and CAM-type precision automatic lathes to process iron, steel, stainless steel, copper, aluminum and titanium.

We specialize in producing a variety of metal hardware components and electronic connectors used in vehicles like cars, motorcycles, scooters and bicycles, as well as hydraulic and pneumatic fittings — combining competitive pricing with dependable, on-time delivery.
