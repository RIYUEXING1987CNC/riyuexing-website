About Us

A trusted Taiwan CNC manufacturer since 1987

RI YUE XING Precision Industrial Co., Ltd. has specialized in precision CNC turning for over three decades. We operate Swiss-type CNC turning, automatic lathes, CNC milling and secondary operations to produce small, high-tolerance components for global customers.

We work with stainless steel, brass, copper and aluminum, delivering parts in the Ø2mm–Ø20mm range with tolerances as tight as ±0.01mm — built for industries where precision is non-negotiable.
